Pancakes- the Holy Grail of Breakfast

fluffy or crepe-like, sweet or savory, pancakes are the perfect breakfast food

Tools

  • 2 mixing bowls
  • whisk
  • ladle or cup measure
  • griddle or large frying pan

Ingredients

wet ingredients

  • 8 oz. milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 stick melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • extra butter for cooking

dry ingredients

  • 8 oz flour (1 1/3-1 1/2 cups)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Combine wet ingredients in a bowl, whisk until thoroughly mixed
  2. Combine dry ingredients
  3. Mix wet and dry ingredients just enough to combine. Add 1-2 oz milk if you like thinner pancakes
  4. Prepare frying pan or griddle by setting it over medium heat, greasing pan with oil, bacon fat, or butter
  5. When pan is hot enough that the fat sizzles, dollop batter in ~1/4 cup drops, cook until fluffy
  6. store cooked pancakes in a warming tray until batch is finished, then serve and enjoy!

makes about 8 pancakes/4 servings
