Huevos Rancheros- A Whole Damn Meal

If you need a little bit of every taste for breakfast- spicy, acidic, salty, fatty, carb-y- then huevos rancheros are the breakfast for you.

Tools

  • 2 skillets
  • serving plate

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 15.5oz can black beans
  • 1/2 cup salsa (can use tomato salsa or salsa verde)
  • 2 small corn tortillas
  • ~2 oz of cotija cheese or queso fresco, crumbled
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • hot sauce to taste

Optional Toppings:

  • small white or red onion, diced
  • chopped cilantro
  • fresh jalapeno, diced
  • avocado slices
  • lime wedge

Directions

  1. Add olive oil and cumin to skillet, saute on low heat for 1 min
  2. Add salsa, increase heat and cook until salsa starts to thicken
  3. Add beans and a little bit of water, salt, and pepper cook until thoroughly warmed
  4. Heat tortillas- in the microwave under wet paper towel for 15s on high, or on another skillet on low heat ~3min per side
  5. Fry 2 eggs sunny side up (about 5 min)
  6. Have all garnishes at the ready
  7. Spoon salsa mixture onto tortillas. Slide eggs from pan onto salsa as soon as the eggs are done
  8. Add garnishes, possibly more salt and pepper, eat immediately (will be messy but very worth it)

makes 1 serving
